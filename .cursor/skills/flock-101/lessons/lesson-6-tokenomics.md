# Lesson 6: FLock Tokenomics

## The Backbone

The **FLOCK token** is what makes the whole decentralised system work. It powers all three elements of the FLock ecosystem:

| Element | Token Role |
|---------|-----------|
| **AI Arena** | Staking for Training Nodes and Validators; rewards for top models |
| **FL Alliance** | Staking commitment; slash mechanism for malicious behaviour |
| **AI Marketplace** | Payment for API calls; model hosting stakes |

## Why Tokenomics Matters

In a centralised system, a company pays for compute and hires engineers. In FLock's decentralised system, you need:

1. **Quality gate** — Stake to prevent spam/low-quality work → **Staking**
2. **Honesty incentive** — Reward good work, punish bad work → **Slash mechanism**
3. **Fair distribution** — Rewards proportional to contribution → **Emission allocation**

Without proper tokenomics, a decentralised AI system devolves into chaos or gets captured by bad actors.

## gmFLOCK — The Game Token

**gmFLOCK** is FLock's gaming token mechanism for AI Arena specifically:

- **Transferable** — Can be bought/sold
- **Restricted use** — Can ONLY be used for: staking as Training Nodes, Validators, or Delegators
- **Not for speculation** — The restriction is intentional to prevent purely financial speculation from disrupting the training economy

## The Slash Mechanism

If a participant (Training Node or Validator) behaves dishonestly:

1. Their staked tokens are **slashed** (burned or distributed to honest participants)
2. They may be **banned** from future tasks
3. Their **reputation score** drops

Slash conditions include:
- Submitting plagiarised or copied models
- Colluding with other participants
- Submitting obviously wrong validation scores
- Going offline / failing to complete assigned tasks

## Emission Rewards

FLock has an **emission system** — new tokens are created and distributed to incentivise participation during the platform's growth phase.

Rewards are distributed based on:
- Task performance ranking
- Stake size
- Validator scoring
- Delegator contributions

This emission schedule is designed to bootstrap the ecosystem while ensuring long-term sustainability.
