# Lesson 8: How to Participate

## Paths Based on Your Background

### For Developers / Data Scientists

You can participate as a **Training Node**:
1. Visit [train.flock.io](https://train.flock.io)
2. Stake FLOCK tokens
3. Choose an active task
4. Submit your trained model
5. Earn rewards if your model ranks well

**Prerequisites:** ML training skills, GPU compute (can rent from cloud providers)

### For ML Validators

You can participate as a **Validator**:
1. Stake FLOCK tokens
2. Get assigned validation tasks
3. Score submitted models honestly
4. Earn validation rewards

**Prerequisites:** Understanding of model evaluation metrics, stake tokens

### For Investors / Token Holders

You can participate as a **Delegator**:
1. Delegate your FLOCK to a trusted Training Node or Validator
2. Earn a share of their rewards
3. Choose delegatees based on their track record and reward share ratio

**Prerequisites:** FLOCK tokens, research into delegatee performance

### For Businesses / Organisations

You can participate through:
- **FL Alliance** — Contribute private data to improve AI models
- **AI Marketplace** — License models for your products
- **Task Creation** (coming soon) — Define AI tasks for the community to solve

## The 2026 Roadmap

FLock has an active development roadmap:
- Opening task creation to the community
- More model types and task categories
- Expanding FL Alliance to more industries
- Improved delegation UI and tooling
- Cross-chain expansion

## Your Personal Participation Plan

**Exercise:** Based on your background, answer these questions:

1. What is your primary skill? (coding / data science / investment / business)
2. Do you have access to GPU compute? (yes / no / cloud only)
3. How much capital do you want to commit? (for staking/delegation)
4. What domain interests you? (healthcare / finance / general AI)

**Example answers:**
- Developer + GPU: → Become a Training Node
- Data scientist + no GPU: → Become a Validator or Delegator
- Investor + low technical skill: → Delegator
- Business with private data: → FL Alliance participant
