### Question 21: Difference between AI Arena and FL Alliance
**Maximum: 5 points**

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Correctly identifies AI Arena as competitive model training and FL Alliance as collaborative fine-tuning with proprietary data |
| 3-4 | Mentions both but doesn't fully capture the competitive vs collaborative distinction |
| 1-2 | One concept correct but other is vague or wrong |
| 0 | Neither concept correctly explained |

**Sample 5-point answer:** "AI Arena is where developers compete to build the best AI models on public datasets in a Kaggle-style competition, while FL Alliance is where those champion models are further improved through collaborative fine-tuning with proprietary data that never leaves its source."

### Question 22: Why staking is necessary
**Maximum: 5 points**

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Clearly explains staking as both a quality gate (prevents spam) and a commitment mechanism (ensures honest participation with economic penalty for misbehaviour) |
| 3-4 | Mentions quality gate OR commitment but not both |
| 1-2 | Vague reference to "ensuring quality" without understanding the economic penalty mechanism |
| 0 | Incorrect answer |

**Sample 5-point answer:** "Staking ensures Training Nodes and Validators have real economic skin in the game — if they produce low-quality work or behave dishonestly, their staked tokens get slashed, creating a financial incentive to act in the network's best interest."
